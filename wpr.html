Winter Public Relations

Winter Public Relations is a boutique public relations firm specializing in strategic PR and marketing campaigns.

Role

To create a visually appealing and cohensive marketing website that is elegant with a subtle color palette and added pops of color. The owner wanted to maintain the site going forward so a very user friendly platform was utlized, WIX.

Technology

Illustrator / Wix

PROJECT DESCRIPTION

Winter Public Relations is a thriving PR firm that works directly with clients as well as collaborating with other agencies to design compelling campaigns. For this project, the narrative content was provided to me. I worked to structure the information in consumable chunks and visually differentiate direct client and agency support work. Working within a simple and neutral color palette required careful use of font styles, shapes, dimention, object size and continuity. The result is a desktop and mobile friendly presence that is beautiful.
