Pockets

Pockets is a stylish apron that provides, well, more pockets to carry the items you need to be prepared for your job. It's a new company looking to finalize their marketing presence, create interest in the product and provide an on-line ordering tool for word of mouth visitors.

Role

Marketing support to articulate form and function and then craft a current and relevent visual design showcasing the fun and stylish practicality of Pockets. Also, to design and highlight the personalities of the people behind the product.

Technology

Illustrator / Balsamiq / InVision / Peek User / Testing / Sketch
