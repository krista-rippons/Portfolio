School Deets

School Deets streamlines communications between schools and parents with multilingual capabilities, a dedicated mobile app and an easy to use Communication Center for adminsitration. All within a private, secure and Google integrated platform.

Role

Product evaluation with focus on user experience, guidance on Minimal Viable Product,
product strategy/roadmap creation and project management.

Technology

Snagit / Sketch / draw.io / Camtasia / Trello / WordPress

PROJECT APPROACH

When I joined the School Deets team they were well on their way to a soft launch. The product was about 90% through the development cycle yet desparate functionality was hindering completion. I was the fresh pair of eyes to help articulate priorities and define critical functionality for a clean Minimum Viable Product.

Together we inventoried the product to determine what was working, not working, missing, redundant or not intuitive. Armed with a fresh current state analysis, we quickly identified a core data relationship that needed some refinment. We set critical path milestones and tweaked the project management tool, Trello, to more efficiently sync with the development team using short sprints and prioritied requests.

PROJECT FOCUS

It was time to get to work and visually define the problem as well as present a solution. Current state screen shots and desired future state mock-ups made the communication with development very effective. We were able to quickly brainstorm solutions based on development capacity weighed with critical path priority. Several iterations were necessary as we continued to simplify.

I worked with the founding partner to strategize product development and outline a product roadmap. Also, I prepared future change orders and designed additional functionality that will be phased in and weighted against user feedback.

ADDITIONAL CONTRIBUTIONS

Some of the other projects I consulted on included writing scope of work for development of mobile app, preparation of training materials and the initial scoping for several fundamental changes to revamp User Roles, Data Relationships and Reporting functionality.

PROJECT RESULTS

School Deets had a succcessful MVP alpha launch the summer of 2016 with more schools on-boarding in the fall of 2016!
