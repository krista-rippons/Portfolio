Fresh Wave Creative

Fresh Wave Creative is a newly formed boutique content marketing firm looking for an on line presence.

Role

Build a responsive website to market new service business. We had an opportunity to define the service offering and articulate the value proposition in context of the competitive analysis findings. Project scope included user research, logo creation, visual design and development of the final product.

Technology

Illustrator / Photoshop / Balsamiq / InVision / Peek User Testing / HTML / CSS / GitHub / SourceTree

PROJECT APPROACH

Fresh Wave Creative is a small business in the early stages of building business beyond word of mouth referrals. As the project scope was defined it became clear that designing the website would begin with a branding and identity definition project.

As with any project, I start by learning about the subject matter. AI began looking to other sites for inspiration...boutique marketing and PR firms, design studios and creative agencies. Visually... Got specific about competitive analysis. What was the service offering? What qualities were highlighted most frequently?

IDEATION

Paired research with Sara's unique qualifications and personal style to determine what should be highlighted. Created buzz words and started ideation of turning those words into visual design. Color palette calm, playful, creative but simple.
Defined User Personas which began to shape the messaging. Also, ensuring we had a great project example.
